Welcome to Brew & Go

About Us

Brew & Go Coffee is a local Kuching-based brand founded in 2023. Starting as a home-based coffee business, we opened our first kiosk at One Jaya Mall and expanded to a second kiosk at Plaza Merdeka Shopping Centre in 2024. We serve handcrafted coffee, matcha, chocolate, and other non-coffee beverages. Our goal is to bring convenient, quality drinks to the heart of the community.
